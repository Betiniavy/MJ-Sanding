About MJ Sanding

MJ Sanding is a trusted floor sanding business based in Wallington, founded by two close friends with a shared passion for quality workmanship and great customer service.

With over a decade of experience in both Scandinavia and the UK, we bring a unique, detail-driven approach to every floor we restore. We understand how important beautiful, durable flooring is for your home or business, which is why we treat every project with the same care and attention as if it were our own.

At MJ Sanding, we believe great flooring starts with listening. By taking the time to understand your space, lifestyle, and expectations, we’re able to recommend the right solutions, materials, and finishes to achieve long-lasting results that genuinely suit your home or commercial space.

Since 2012, our goal has been simple: to deliver exceptional results with honesty, transparency, and respect for our clients’ spaces. We’re proud to offer fair and flexible pricing, clear communication throughout every stage, and dust-controlled sanding methods that help keep your property clean and disruption to a minimum.

Whether it’s a single room or a large-scale renovation, we take pride in every job, no matter the size or location across the UK. We’re always happy to discuss your requirements, answer questions, and help you make informed decisions about the best finish for your floors.

Beyond sanding and restoration, we also provide underfloor insulation solutions designed to improve comfort and energy efficiency. This allows us to look at flooring as a complete system helping to reduce heat loss, improve warmth underfoot, and create quieter, more comfortable spaces all year round.

Every project is completed by an experienced team, with a clear, straightforward process from start to finish.
