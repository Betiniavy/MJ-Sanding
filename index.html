Our Story

MJ Sanding is a trusted floor sanding business based in Wallington, founded by two close friends with a shared passion for quality workmanship and great customer service.

With over a decade of experience in both Scandinavia and the UK, we bring a unique, detail-driven approach to every floor we restore. We understand how important beautiful, durable flooring is for your home or business and that’s why we treat every project with the same care and attention as if it were our own.

Since 2012, our goal has been simple: to deliver exceptional results with honesty, transparency, and respect for our clients’ spaces. We’re proud to offer fair and flexible pricing, clear communication, and dust-controlled sanding that keeps your property clean throughout the process.

Whether it’s a single room or a large-scale renovation, we take pride in every job no matter the size or location across the UK. We’re always happy to discuss your needs and help you achieve the perfect finish for your floors.
