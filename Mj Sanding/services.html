FAQs

A few common questions about dust, drying times and how the work is carried out.

Is sanding very dusty?

We use modern HEPA extractors and mask doorways/vents. Dust is kept to a minimum and we tidy before we leave.

How long before we can walk on the floor?

Usually after 2–3 hours in socks. Light use the next day; full cure is 3–4 days depending on the product.

What finish do you recommend—oil or lacquer?

Oils look warm and are easy to spot-repair. Lacquers are tougher overall and need less maintenance. We’ll suggest the best match for traffic and look.

Do you move furniture?

We can help with lighter items. Please arrange heavy pieces and empty rooms as much as possible—ask if you need a moving option.

Can you repair gaps and damaged boards?

Yes—gap filling, slivers, loose parquet blocks, and board replacements. We blend repairs to match the surrounding floor.

How long does a typical job take?

Most rooms are 1–2 days. Larger homes, staining, or repairs can add time—we’ll confirm in the quote.

Will there be strong odours?

We use low-odour, low-VOC products wherever possible. Keep windows slightly open during curing for best results.

Do you work weekends?

Weekdays as standard; Weekends, Bank holidays and Night slots are available on request. We’ll plan around access and building rules.
