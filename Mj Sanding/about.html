About MJ Sanding

Trusted floor sanding & restoration specialists serving London and across the UK since 2012.

Our Story

MJ Sanding is a trusted floor sanding business based in Wallington, founded by two close friends with a shared passion for quality workmanship and great customer service.

With over a decade of experience in both Scandinavia and the UK, we bring a unique, detail-driven approach to every floor we restore. We understand how important beautiful, durable flooring is for your home or business.

Since 2012, our goal has been simple: deliver exceptional results with honesty, transparency, and respect for our clients’ spaces. We offer fair pricing, clear communication, and dust-controlled sanding that keeps your property clean throughout the process.

Whether it’s a single room or a large-scale renovation, every project is handled with care, precision, and pride in our craft.
